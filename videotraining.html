Video Training Reviews

Staying current in all your areas of interest can be challenging. I have found PluralSight, YouTube and Cybrary to be great resources for web security, modern web dev and testing. Here are a few courses from each that I have found enlighting. I hope to create individual reviews for each of them some day, for now, here is a simple listing.


PluralSight Courses

Here are just a handful of PluralSight Classes I recommend. I have used PS since around 2012 when I got a free one year subscription while at Microsoft TechEd 2012. My work currently has a corporate license and it is well liked.

Security for Hackers and Developers

Courses by Dr. Jared DeMott cover C++, Fuzzing, Code Analysis, Exploit and Malware Analysis. I hope he makes a course on ROP soon!!!
1) Exploit Dev
2) Reverse Engineering
3) Fuzzing
4) Malware
5) Code Audting

Certified Ethical Hacker

I know the CEH is looked down upon, but the two instructors here do a pretty good job. The material is top notch and helped me setup a methodology and I picked up some knowledge here and there:

Test Driven Development, Testing and Development

There are many good courses here on all areas of dev. I am quite found of the courses that give an overview of the changes of a language or tool. The "What's new" type series.

Cybrary

I asked my work to purchase a subscription, but they have chosen to hold off. Hopefully I can provide a review sometime in the future.
